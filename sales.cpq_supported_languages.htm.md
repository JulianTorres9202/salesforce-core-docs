# Salesforce CPQ-Supported Languages

Salesforce CPQ supports English, Spanish (Mexican), French, German, Japanese, and Brazilian Portuguese. You can change the Salesforce language by editing company settings, but Salesforce CPQ-specific values are translated only for the six CPQ languages. (Salesforce CPQ Managed Package)

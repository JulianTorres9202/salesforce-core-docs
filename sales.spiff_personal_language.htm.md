# Specify Your Preferred Language in Salesforce Spiff

Navigate Salesforce Spiff in your preferred language, except Designer, which is available only in English. Supported languages for all other parts of the app include English, French, German, Japanese, Spanish, and Portuguese. This setting is available to all Spiff roles.

# Enable Translation Workbench

To assist with localizing B2B store information, enable the Translation Workbench and add the languages that you want to use in your stores.

1.  Click  at the top of the page, and then select **Setup**.
2.  In the Quick Find box, enter Translation Workbench, and then select **Translation Language Settings**.
3.  Click **Enable**.
4.  Click **Add**.
5.  From the Language dropdown, select the language and select **Active**.
6.  Save your changes.
    
    Add as many languages as you need for your stores.

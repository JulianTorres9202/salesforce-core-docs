# Inclusive Language Updates

As part of our efforts to refine language in our products to more accurately reflect inclusivity, Salesforce is replacing terms deemed noninclusive.

**Where:** This change applies to all versions of Salesforce B2C Commerce and B2C Commerce services in Production, Sandbox, and Development instances.

**Why:** Equality is a core value at Salesforce. To ensure that language in Salesforce content and products is more inclusive, we’ll continue to make updates and communicate them in the release notes.

**Why:** To learn more about terms that are changing, see [How we’re bringing inclusive language to our products](https://www.salesforce.com/news/stories/how-were-bringing-inclusive-language-to-our-products/).
